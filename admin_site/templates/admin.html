Top Secret Double Chocolate Cookie Recepie

  • 1 c. butter, softened
  • 1-½ c. white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp. vanilla
  • 2 c. flour
  • ⅔ c. cocoa powder
  • ¾ tsp. baking soda
  • ¼ tsp. salt
  • 2 c. semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • ½ c. walnuts

Preheat oven to 350°F, combine ingredients in bowl, bake 8-10 minutes.
